Vision

We believe that a good school should add more to excellent performance in academics. To be a world-class educational institution providing qualitative, comprehensive and standard education. Understanding and appreciating life in all aspects is also of equal importance. Each child must be prepared to restore sanity and dignity back into our society and in no time Nigeria will be great again. This is our vision, May God help us.

Mission

Every Child is born with abilities,talent that will make a child successful in life. Our Mission is to enlighten,nurture and educate the child on the abilities and talent in order for the child to be able utilize it toward achieving a fulfilled life

Philosophy

The School is a non-partisan, non-denominational and non-discriminatory Institution. The criteria for admitting students include; good academic performance at the entry point and willingness of the candidates to achieve excellent results through consistent hard work and sound morals.
